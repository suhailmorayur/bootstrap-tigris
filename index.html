A Sanctuary for Tigers and Biodiversity Conservation

In the heart of Tigris lies a sanctuary committed to the conservation of tigers and biodiversity. Our mission extends beyond protecting the majestic felines to safeguarding the entire ecosystem. Learn about our initiatives, partnerships, and the tireless efforts driving Tigris to be a beacon of hope for endangered species and their habitats.

WWWF 2024 Award

Tigris proudly stands as the recipient of the prestigious WWF 2024 Award, a testament to our unwavering commitment to tiger conservation. This accolade recognizes our dedication to preserving biodiversity and fostering sustainable practices within the reserve. We are honored to be acknowledged for our contributions towards creating a thriving environment for these magnificent creatures

180 tigers saved

Tigris celebrates a monumental achievement with the successful rescue and protection of 180 tigers within its sanctuary. Through rigorous conservation eff orts, we have secured the future of these magnificent creatures, ensuring their well-being and contributing significantly to the global initiative for the preservation of endangered species.

2M+ visiters

Tigris has welcomed over 2 million visitors, fostering a community passionate about wildlife conservation. Our reserve serves as an educational hub, inspiring individuals to connect with nature and contribute to the ongoing efforts to protect endangered species. Each visitor becomes a crucial advocate, amplifying the impact of our collective commitment to environmental stewardship.

14 Services

Tigris offers a comprehensive range of 14 services designed to enhance the visitor experience and support our conservation mission. From guided safaris and educational programs to habitat restoration initiatives, these services form the backbone of our commitment to creating a sustainable and enriching environment for both wildlife and visitors alike.
